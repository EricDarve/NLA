- Very useful in proofs to derive upper bounds, e.g., when analyzing the error or the convergence rate of a numerical algorithm
$$
| x^T y | \le \|x\|_2 \; \|y\|_2
$$
- **Exercise:** you can prove the triangle inequality using CS:
$$
\| x+y \|_2 \le \|x\|_2 + \|y\|_2
$$
